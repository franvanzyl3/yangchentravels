Cultural

Bhutan is a beautiful country below the Himalayan mountain range, landlocked between China and India. Bhutan-the last paradise on the earth reflects age-old religion, culture, arts & architecture in its golden peaks in the background of deep green valley and hillside.

Trekking

Trekking in Bhutan provides a chance to see beautiful sights of unclimbed snow-covered mountains. Whether you’re seeking a peek of the Himalayas or its long treks, we have it all for you. We offer anything from day hikes/to trekking of varying difficulty levels, suitable for adventure seeking. The hikes provide the authentic experience of this Himalayan country. The ideal time to trek in Bhutan is during mid-March to Mid-May and mid-September to the starting of November. Throughout your trek you will enjoy the company of our licensed trek guide, a cook and a horse man to carry your belongings. Both solo and group trekking can be arranged, to suite your budget and trekking needs.
